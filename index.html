Resident Evil 8: Village

The Horror Continues

Resident Evil 8, also known as Resident Evil Village, takes survival horror to chilling new heights. Players step once again into the shoes of Ethan Winters as he searches for his kidnapped daughter in a mysterious village teeming with deadly creatures and secrets.

Main Villains

Lady Dimitrescu became an instant icon due to her towering presence and gothic style. Alongside her, other lords of the village, like Heisenberg and Donna Beneviento, make the journey terrifying and unforgettable.
